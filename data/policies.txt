# HR Policies

## 1. Leave Policy
- **Annual Leave**: Employees are entitled to 20 days of paid annual leave per year. Annual leave requests must be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance.
- **Sick Leave**: Employees receive 10 days of paid sick leave annually. A medical certificate is required for sick leave exceeding 3 consecutive days.
- **Maternity/Paternity Leave**: 
  - Maternity leave is granted for 12 weeks.
  - Paternity leave is granted for 2 weeks.
- **Public Holidays**: The company observes all national public holidays.
- **Unpaid Leave**: Can be granted on a case-by-case basis subject to manager approval.

## 2. Travel Policy
- **Flight Bookings**: All flights should be booked in Economy Class unless the flight duration exceeds 8 hours, in which case Business Class is permitted upon manager approval.
- **Accommodation**: Hotel stays are capped at $150 per night. Employees must book approved partner hotels when available.
- **Per Diem**: A daily allowance of $50 is provided for meals and incidentals during business travel.
- **Reimbursement**: All travel expenses must be submitted within 30 days of returning. Original receipts are mandatory for any expense above $25.
- **Local Transport**: Use of public transport or economy ride-sharing options (like UberX) is encouraged. Rental cars require prior approval.

## 3. Remote Work Policy
- Employees are allowed to work remotely for up to 2 days a week, subject to team requirements and manager approval.
- Core hours (10:00 AM to 3:00 PM local time) must be strictly observed while working remotely.

## 4. Code of Conduct
- All employees are expected to maintain professional behavior and a respectful workplace environment.
- Discrimination or harassment of any kind is strictly prohibited and will result in disciplinary action.
